General Council Meetings
The JKJAC convenes its General Council – bringing together representatives from member political parties, social organizations, and trade unions – to deliberate on the current political situation, approve action plans, and issue joint resolutions.
Bandhs & Protest Days
We call for peaceful shutdowns (bandhs) and observed days across Jammu and Kashmir to protest against policies that undermine our special status, challenge unilateral decisions, and demonstrate the collective will of the people.
Solidarity Rallies & Marches
JKJAC organizes public rallies and marches in major cities and towns to mobilize citizens, express solidarity with political prisoners, and keep the flame of our legitimate rights burning in the public square.
Public Awareness Seminars
We regularly hold public lectures, seminars, and discussions featuring senior leaders, legal experts, and journalists to educate the community on critical issues, constitutional matters, and the importance of a united political stance.

Past Events

A History of
United Struggle

The Jammu Kashmir Joint Action Committee has a long and consistent history of leading the political movement through mass mobilization and advocacy.

Resistance Conference 2024

JKJAC convened a landmark All Parties Conference in Srinagar, attended by senior leaders from all major political constituents. The conference passed a unified resolution rejecting any efforts to alter the demographic and constitutional status of the state.

UN Human Rights Advocacy 2024

A delegation of JKJAC representatives traveled to Geneva to present a memorandum to the UN Human Rights Council, detailing human rights concerns and the political situation on the ground, and appealing to the international community to fulfill its moral obligations.

Cross-District Mobilization 2023

The JKJAC successfully organized a series of public meetings across all 20 districts, aiming to counter divisive narratives and reinforce a unified public mandate for the restoration of our rights and special guarantees.

Diaspora Engagement Tour 2023

JKJAC leaders undertook a tour of key cities in the UK and Europe, engaging with Kashmiri diaspora organizations to brief them on the on-ground situation, coordinate advocacy efforts, and strengthen the global network of solidarity.
