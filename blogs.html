Understanding the Jammu Kashmir Joint Awami Action Committee (JKJAAC)

The Jammu Kashmir Joint Awami Action Committee (JKJAAC), often simply called the JAAC, has emerged as a formidable force in the socio-political landscape of Azad Jammu and Kashmir (AJK). Over the past few years, this coalition has shifted from localized protests to becoming a key negotiator with the federal government, championing issues of economic justice, governance reforms, and political rights. This blog post delves into the origins, demands, and journey of the JKJAAC.
